Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe

Description

Indulge in the nostalgic delight of homemade Peanut Butter Cookies with this simple and irresistible recipe. These cookies strike the perfect balance between chewy and crumbly, with a rich peanut butter flavor that will transport you back to your fondest childhood memories. Whether you're a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe guarantees a batch of cookies that will have everyone asking for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Additional granulated sugar for rolling (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone baking mats.
  2. Cream the Butter and Sugars: In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. This can be done with a hand mixer or stand mixer.
  3. Incorporate Peanut Butter and Eggs: Add in the creamy peanut butter and mix until well combined. Then, add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the pure vanilla extract.
  4. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the peanut butter mixture, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  5. Form Cookie Dough Balls: Scoop out tablespoon-sized portions of cookie dough and roll them into balls. For an extra touch, roll each ball in granulated sugar to add a sweet and crunchy coating.
  6. Arrange on Baking Sheets: Place the cookie dough balls onto the prepared baking sheets, leaving enough space between each cookie to allow for spreading during baking.
  7. Bake to Perfection: Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are golden brown. Keep in mind that the cookies will continue to firm up as they cool, so avoid overbaking.
  8. Cool and Enjoy: Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. Once cooled, these delightful Peanut Butter Cookies are ready to be enjoyed with a glass of milk or your favorite hot beverage.
