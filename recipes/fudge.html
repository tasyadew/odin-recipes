Hot Chocolate Fudge Recipe

Description

Indulge in the ultimate winter treat with our decadent Hot Chocolate Fudge. This rich and creamy delight combines the irresistible flavors of classic hot chocolate with the velvety texture of fudge, creating a heavenly combination that will warm your soul on chilly days. Perfect for holiday gatherings, cozy nights by the fire, or simply satisfying your sweet tooth cravings, this Hot Chocolate Fudge is a delightful fusion of two beloved treats.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup mini marshmallows
  • 1/4 cup crushed candy canes (optional, for a festive touch)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Pan: Line an 8-inch square baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on two opposite sides. This will make it easier to lift the fudge out later.
  2. Melt the Chocolate Mixture: In a medium-sized saucepan over low heat, combine the chocolate chips, sweetened condensed milk, and butter. Stir continuously until the mixture is smooth and well combined.
  3. Add Cocoa Powder and Flavorings: Stir in the cocoa powder, vanilla extract, and salt. Continue to cook and stir until the mixture is glossy and everything is fully incorporated.
  4. Incorporate Marshmallows: Gently fold in the mini marshmallows, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the mixture.
  5. Transfer to the Pan: Pour the hot chocolate fudge mixture into the prepared baking pan, spreading it out evenly with a spatula.
  6. Add Optional Toppings Sprinkle crushed candy canes over the top if you desire a festive touch. Press them lightly into the fudge.
  7. Chill the Fudge: Refrigerate the fudge for at least 2-3 hours or until it is completely set.
  8. Cut and Serve: Once set, use the parchment paper overhang to lift the fudge out of the pan. Place it on a cutting board and cut it into bite-sized squares.
