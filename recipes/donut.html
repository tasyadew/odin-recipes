Krispy Kreme Original Glazed Donut Recipe

Description

Indulge in the heavenly delight of homemade Krispy Kreme Original Glazed Donuts. These melt-in-your-mouth treats are a perfect blend of light, fluffy dough and a luscious sugar glaze. Recreate the iconic taste of Krispy Kreme right in your own kitchen with this easy-to-follow recipe. Whether for a special occasion or a weekend treat, these donuts are guaranteed to bring joy to your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup warm water (110°F/43°C)
  • 3/4 cup warm milk (110°F/43°C)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • Vegetable oil
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough: In a bowl, combine warm milk and sugar. Sprinkle yeast over the mixture and let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy. In a large mixing bowl, combine the activated yeast mixture, salt, softened butter, and egg. Gradually add the flour, one cup at a time, until a soft dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours, or until doubled in size.
  2. Shape and Cut the Donuts: Roll out the dough on a floured surface to about 1/2-inch thickness. Use a round cutter to cut out donuts, and then use a smaller cutter for the donut holes. Place the cut donuts on a floured baking sheet and let them rise for an additional 30 minutes.
  3. Fry the Donuts: Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large, deep skillet to 350°F (175°C). Carefully fry the donuts in batches for about 1-2 minutes per side, or until golden brown. Remove the donuts with a slotted spoon and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  4. Prepare the Glaze: In a bowl, whisk together powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth. Dip each cooled donut into the glaze, ensuring they are fully coated. Place the glazed donuts on a wire rack to allow any excess glaze to drip off.
