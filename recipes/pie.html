Pumpkin Pie Recipe

Description

Indulge in the warm, comforting embrace of a classic Pumpkin Pie that embodies the essence of autumn. This delightful dessert is a celebration of rich, velvety pumpkin filling encased in a buttery, flaky crust. Each bite is a symphony of cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves, harmonizing to create a flavor profile that captures the spirit of the season. Whether served at a holiday feast or as a sweet treat on a crisp evening, this Pumpkin Pie is sure to become a cherished favorite.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the Filling:

  • 1 can (15 ounces) pure pumpkin puree
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup evaporated milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Prepare the Crust: In a food processor, combine flour, sugar, and salt. Add chilled, cubed butter and pulse until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Gradually add ice water, one tablespoon at a time, pulsing until the dough starts to come together. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface, knead gently, and shape it into a disc. Wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Roll Out the Crust: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). On a floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a circle about 12 inches in diameter. Carefully transfer the rolled-out crust to a 9-inch pie dish, gently pressing it into the bottom and sides. Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges for a decorative finish.
  3. Prepare the Filling: In a large bowl, whisk together pumpkin puree, sugar, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, cloves, and salt until well combined. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in evaporated milk and vanilla extract, ensuring a smooth and homogeneous mixture.
  4. Assemble and Bake: Pour the pumpkin filling into the prepared pie crust. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes or until the center is set. Allow the pie to cool completely before serving. For an extra touch, top with whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
